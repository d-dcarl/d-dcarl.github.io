Overview

A business card showcasing my work at GDC 2024. A trading card that I designed and illustrated to use with Augmented Reality and getting myself out there as an artist and designer.

My Role

A personal project I used to challenge myself and learn new skills.

  • Used Unreal Engine 5 sequencer to create art and shots for the Augmented Reality experience
  • Used Adobe Illustrator to create the trading card
  • Printed and cut trading cards myself applying gloss and texture to the card

Tech Stack

  • Unreal Engine 5
  • Blueprints
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Aero

Challenge & Solution

One of the biggest challenges was creating procedurally generated levels that felt hand-crafted and intentional. The solution involved developing a room-based generation system with pre-designed room templates that could be combined in various ways.

Another challenge was balancing difficulty progression as players advanced through the dungeon. I implemented a dynamic difficulty scaling system that adjusted based on player performance.

Results

The prototype was well-received in playtesting, with players particularly enjoying the combat mechanics and level variety. Key metrics showed high engagement and replayability.
